Healdsburg

Upscale town square, Michelin-starred restaurants, boutique wineries, and Russian River access. Premium real estate in the heart of wine country.

Sonoma Valley

Historic plaza, walkable downtown, renowned wineries, and family-friendly neighborhoods. The birthplace of California wine.

Petaluma

Victorian architecture, river town charm, excellent schools, and easy Bay Area access. More affordable entry point to Sonoma living.

Sebastopol

Arts community, apple orchards, farmers markets, and eclectic downtown. Bohemian atmosphere meets wine country lifestyle.

Santa Rosa

County seat with urban amenities, diverse neighborhoods, shopping, dining, and cultural venues. Central location for exploring the region.

Sonoma Coast

Dramatic coastline, rugged beaches, cool climate wines, and stunning views. Peaceful retreat from wine country bustle.
